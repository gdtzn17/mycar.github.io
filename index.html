Hi everyone and welcome to my garage. In here, I'll be posting pictures and progress of my car that I am working on. I put so much emphasis on aesthetics to make it more "European" as well as tweaking the performance. Its a way on how to impress other enthusiasts within the car community. I have some experience on working and repairing my car but I let my mechanic friends to deal with the heavy work, such as suspension, engine, electronics, etc. Unfortunately, funding for this project can be an issue.



Basic Info of my car
Model: 2004 Volkswagen Jetta GLI Sedan
Engine: 2.8L VR6 24v "BDF"
Power (Factory): 200HP (@ 6200 RPM) with 195ft/lbs of torque (@ 3200 RPM)
Power (With modifications): 216HP with 211ft/lbs of torque
Transmission: 6-Speed Manual Gear box
Drivetrain: Front Wheel Drive


History of my car (chronological order)

  1. The car was purchased on August of 2009 from a reposessed dealership. I'm the second owner.
  2. Some major setbacks after purchase:
    • Oil pan cracked
    • Rusted battery terminal
    • Cracked thermostat housing
    • CV boot on driver-side axle was torn
  3. 2 months after purchase, vehicle was repaired:
    • Oil pan replaced
    • New battery terminal
    • New battery
    • New Thermostat housing
  4. November 2011, hydraulic master cylinder for manual gear box became faulty.
  5. December 2011, hydraulic master cylinder was replaced along with new OEM clutch system. Replaced PCV, breather hose, and EVAP vacuum line.
  6. Spring 2012, started on modifying the aesthetics such as customized front grille, sidemarkers, and roof rack.
  7. Summer 2012, replaced passenger side axle. The insides and CV joint was refreshed.
  8. Spring 2014, suspected oil leak from engine bay. Repaired and replaced head valve cover gasket.
  9. Summer 2014, replaced headlights with European aftermarket ones, replaced front lip, and tailights.
  10. Spring 2015, replaced driver side axle.
  11. Summer 2015, overhauled entire suspension system with new OEM components and aftermarket coil-overs.
  12. Spring 2016, purchased and installed catback exhaust system.
  13. July 2016, Check Engine Light (CEL) came on. Throttle issues and position crankshaft sensor faulty. Installed new throttle body and position crankshaft sensor.
  14. August 2016, ECU has been remapped with the Unitronic Stage 1 performance program. Installed aftermarket "dogbone" mount.
  15. October 2016, replaced alternator and tension pulley.
  16. April 2017, CEL came on. Misfire in cylinder 1. Replaced all six coilpacks.
  17. Fall 2017, purchased and installed 18" VW R32 Aristo wheels.
  18. Spring 2018, repaired entire cooling system. Replaced engine wiring harness. Installed aftermarket aluminum cooling pipe.
  19. December 2019, replaced stock air intake system with Autotech Cold Air Intake (CAI). Replaced driver side door controls.
  20. January 2021, CEL came on. Numerous emission codes.
  21. March 2021, ran test with smoke machine and VAGCOM (VW/Audi diagnostics software). EVAP vacuum lines and brake booster hose cracked.
  22. April 2021, repaired cracked hoses. Replaced throttle body and gas cap. No CEL present.
